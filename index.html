Our Story

Where Kathmandu Plays

Busy Futsal — व्यस्त फुटसल — was born from a simple idea: every player in Kathmandu deserves a world-class surface to play on. Nestled in Link Marg, we've built more than just a futsal court — we've built a community.

Our lush artificial turf, professional markings, and welcoming environment make us the go-to destination for casual kickabouts, team practices, and friendly tournaments across the Valley.

We believe football is for everyone — from schoolkids to professionals, from early morning joggers to late-evening warriors. Our mission is to make quality futsal accessible and affordable for all.

Our Facilities

World-Class Turf

Premium Court

Premium Turf

FIFA-standard artificial grass designed for speed and control. Low maintenance, high performance — the turf you'll want to play on every day.

Daily Open Slots

Available every day of the week from early morning till 8 PM. Whether it's a quick game after school or a weekend tournament, we've got your slot ready.

Safety Fencing

Full perimeter fencing keeps the ball in play and provides a secure environment for players of all ages. Play hard, play safe.

5v5 Format

Optimized court dimensions for fast-paced 5-a-side action. Perfect for small teams, corporate events, or just picking up a game.

Parking Available

On-site parking for bikes and cars. Come as a team, park with ease, focus on the game — not the logistics.
